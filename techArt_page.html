Tech Art Showcase

A collection of some of my best shaders and VFX.

All of these shaders were written using the unity shader graph, visual effect graph, and particle system. The GIF on the left shows some of these shaders in context of a small Asteroids like game.

These shaders turned out great. Especially with how well they merge together in the final product, forming a coheasive gameplay experience.

Due to GIF compression being lossy, all the GIFs have some artifacting that is not present when the shader used in game.



Nebula Shader

While one may assume its just a png this nebula is actually generated procedurally by a shader. It also has the added effect where the brighter the pixel the closer to the camera it gets. This gives the nebula some slight paralax when moving making it look much more interesting.

Card Effects

These shaders were written for a rouge-like chess game I worked on in a 18 person team.

Model Effects

These shaders were written the same a rouge-like chess game as the cards. They are for the pieces on the board and used when summoning, highlighting, and enchanting pieces.
