Making Refraction

Refraction is one of my favorite and most challenging projects I've worked on yet. Due to the contraints of the assignment I had to write the entire lighting engine on the cpu.
How it works:
Step 1: All objects and tilesets are merged together into one image (the normal maps are also merged into a seperate image).
Step 2: For each light we then go over each pixel in its range and calcuate the lights strength, while using the normal map to factor in the surface normal.
Step 3: The individual lights strength are added together resulting in the total brightness. Step 4: We then apply any post prossessing effects, in this case the simulated scanlines and minimum brightness.
